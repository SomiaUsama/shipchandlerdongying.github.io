The
Story

Hello, we are Dongying Co., Ltd., your trusted ship chandlers dedicated to serving the unique needs of the maritime industry. Our mission is to ensure every vessel is fully equipped for its journey, providing a comprehensive range of supplies and services to meet all maritime requirements.

Whether it’s essentials for a cargo ship or specialized gear for research vessels, our commitment is unwavering. Each order is handled promptly, ensuring you have what you need, when you need it. Join us in navigating the waters with confidence.

Join us at Dongying Co., Ltd., and navigate the seas with confidence, knowing that you have a dependable partner by your side.

What We Do

Services

Provisioning Supplies

At Dongying Co., Ltd., we provide a comprehensive range of provisioning supplies to ensure that your vessel is fully equipped for every journey. From fresh produce and dry goods to beverages and personal care products, we cater to all your crew’s needs with high-quality items.

Safety Equipment

At Dongying Co., Ltd., safety is our top priority. We supply a wide range of safety equipment designed to protect your crew and ensure the safe operation of your vessel. From life jackets and fire safety gear to emergency signaling devices and first aid kits, we provide high-quality products that meet international safety standards.

Deck and Engine Supplies

At Dongying Co., Ltd., we offer a comprehensive selection of deck and engine supplies to ensure the smooth operation and maintenance of your vessel. Our products include ropes, chains, engine parts, lubricants, and tools designed to withstand the demanding conditions of the maritime environment.

Navigational Equipment

We understand the importance of precise and reliable equipment when navigating through various conditions, and that's why we offer products from trusted manufacturers known for their accuracy and durability. Whether you need to upgrade existing equipment or replace worn-out devices, we are committed to delivering top-notch navigational supplies to keep your vessel on course.
